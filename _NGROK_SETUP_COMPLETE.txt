❓ FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
═════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Q: Do I really need Ngrok?
A: No, optional. Use if you want remote access.

Q: Why do URLs change?
A: Free plan gets new URLs each restart.
   Pro plan: static URLs ($5/month).

Q: Is it safe?
A: Yes! HTTPS encryption, auth tokens, secure.

Q: Can I access from my phone?
A: Yes! Works on any device with a browser.

Q: What if it disconnects?
A: Just restart. A-Bot keeps running locally.

Q: Do I need to restart A-Bot?
A: No. A-Bot stays running, just loses internet access.

Q: How much does it cost?
A: Free plan is great!
   Pro plan: ~$5/month for static URLs.

Q: How do I stop Ngrok?
A: Press Ctrl+C in PowerShell window.
