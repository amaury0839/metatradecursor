Q: Do I need Ngrok?
A: No, it's optional. You can run A-Bot locally without it.
   Use Ngrok if you want to access A-Bot from other devices/internet.

Q: Why do the URLs change?
A: Free plan gives dynamic URLs. They change each time you restart.
   Paid plans offer static URLs that never change.

Q: Is it safe to expose my bot?
A: Yes! Ngrok creates secure HTTPS tunnels. All traffic is encrypted.
   Still, don't expose sensitive information or trade heavily on public networks.

Q: Can I use it from my phone?
A: Yes! Just open the public URL on your phone's browser.
   Your A-Bot Dashboard will work on any device.

Q: What if I want to keep it private?
A: Add basic auth:
     ngrok http 8504 --basic-auth "username:password"
   Now only people with username/password can access.

Q: Can I see who's accessing my bot?
A: Yes! Check http://localhost:4040 while Ngrok is running.
   Shows all requests, headers, responses, etc.

Q: What if Ngrok disconnects?
A: Restart it with the same command.
   Your local A-Bot keeps running fine.

Q: How much does Ngrok cost?
A: Free plan is great for testing!
   Pro plan ~$5/month for static URLs and more features.
